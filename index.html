How It Works

The Cirelli's Process

01

Consultation

We assess your vehicle's condition, discuss your goals, and recommend the right treatment package for your needs and budget.

02

Decontamination

A full wash, clay bar treatment, and surface decontamination to prepare the paint for correction and coating.

03

Correction

Machine polishing to eliminate imperfections, swirls, and scratches — revealing the true depth and gloss of your paint.

04

Protection

Ceramic coating applied to lock in the results and protect your investment for years to come. The showroom moment.
