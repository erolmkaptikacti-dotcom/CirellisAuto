What We Do

Crafted for
every vehicle

From daily drivers to collector classics, motorcycles and beyond — every vehicle that enters our shop receives the same obsessive attention to detail. No shortcuts. No compromises.

1

Ceramic Coating

A semi-permanent shield chemically bonded to your clear coat. Repels water, blocks UV, resists chemical etching, and keeps your paint gleaming for 3 to 7 years — with far less maintenance between washes.

 2

Paint Correction

Professional machine polishing that removes swirl marks, water spots, fine scratches, and oxidation at the microscopic level — restoring the depth and gloss your paint had the day it left the factory.

 3

Full Detail

A top-to-bottom treatment covering every interior and exterior surface — decontaminated, deep-cleaned, and protected. This is the service that earns us 5-star reviews every time.

 4

Motorcycle Detailing

Specialized care built around the unique demands of motorcycles — chrome polishing, engine bay cleaning, spoke detailing, leather conditioning, and optional ceramic coating. Harleys, sport bikes, cruisers, and custom builds welcome.

 5

Interior Detailing

Steam cleaning, shampoo and hot water extraction on carpets and seats, compressed air blow out, leather conditioning, odor elimination, and full vacuum — every surface in your cabin treated with the right product. Leaves your interior looking and smelling the way it did the day you bought it.

 6

Exterior Detailing

Hand wash, clay bar decontamination, iron fallout removal, engine bay cleaning, and trim protection — every painted panel, wheel, and piece of glass left spotless and protected by our ceramic wax.

Every service is tailored to your vehicle's exact needs.

How It Works

The Cirelli's Process

01

Consultation

We start by walking the vehicle with you — assessing the paint condition, identifying problem areas, and understanding what you want to achieve. From there we recommend the right combination of services for your vehicle's needs and your budget. No upselling, no guesswork.

02

Decontamination

Before any correction begins, the paint has to be completely clean. We run a foam cannon pre-wash, thorough hand wash, iron fallout treatment, and full clay bar decontamination — removing the embedded contamination that a standard wash leaves behind. The surface is then dried and inspected so correction starts on a flawless base.

03

Correction

A two-step machine polish process: the first cut removes deeper defects — swirls, scratches, and oxidation — while the second refining pass brings the paint to maximum clarity and gloss. Every panel inspected under high-intensity lighting before moving forward.

04

Protection

A professional-grade ceramic coating is applied to the freshly corrected paint, chemically bonding to the clear coat for a hydrophobic, UV-resistant layer of protection. Available in 3, 5, or 7-year formulations — your results are sealed in and built to last.
