Ceramic Coating

Ceramic coating is the gold standard in paint protection — a professional-grade, semi-permanent layer that chemically bonds to your vehicle's clear coat. Once cured, it creates a hydrophobic surface that repels water, blocks UV rays, resists chemical etching, and makes every wash dramatically easier. The result is paint that stays cleaner longer and holds its gloss for years, not weeks.

Unlike waxes or paint sealants that sit on top of the surface and degrade within months, ceramic coatings become part of the clear coat itself. That means protection you don't have to reapply every season — and a depth of gloss that gets noticed every time you pull up. At our shop, every ceramic coating application starts with a full decontamination and polish prep to maximize adhesion and ensure the coating performs to its full potential. We offer packages ranging from 3-year to 7-year protection, and we'll help you choose the right one for how you use and maintain your vehicle.

What's Included

  • Full decontamination wash & iron fallout removal
  • Clay bar paint decontamination
  • Paint polishing prep to maximize coating adhesion
  • Panel-wipe with IPA to remove all residue
  • Professional ceramic coating application (3, 5, or 7-year formulations)
  • Curing period under controlled conditions
  • Final inspection and gloss assessment
  • Maintenance care instructions & touch-up guidance
3-Year Protection

Our premium multi-layer application with deeper gloss and extended protection. For those who want the best — maximum hardness, longevity, and shine.

5-Year Protection

An advanced multi-layer coating built for long-haul durability. Superior gloss depth, hardness, and chemical resistance — ideal for daily drivers that demand lasting results.

7-Year Protection

Our flagship coating package. Maximum layers, maximum hardness, and the longest protection available. The definitive choice for collectors, enthusiasts, and those who refuse to compromise.

2

Paint Correction

Over time, swirl marks, fine scratches, water spots, and oxidation build up in your clear coat — dulling the finish and killing depth. Paint correction is the only true fix. Using professional dual-action and rotary polishers with cutting compounds and finishing polishes, we systematically remove paint defects at the microscopic level, restoring the clarity and gloss the factory intended. After correction, your car's paint genuinely looks better than when it was new.

Most of the swirls and scratches people see on their paint didn't come from the road — they came from improper washing, automatic car washes, and dry wiping. Paint correction addresses all of that. We take paint thickness readings before and after to ensure safe material removal, and we work under professional lighting to identify every defect before we touch the machine to your paint. Whether your vehicle needs a light one-stage polish or a full multi-stage correction, we'll give you an honest assessment and execute it to the highest standard. Correction can also be paired with a ceramic coating to lock in the results and protect your paint going forward.

What's Included

  • Thorough wash, rinse, and foam decontamination
  • Iron fallout removal & clay bar treatment
  • Paint thickness gauge readings (pre & post)
  • Single or multi-stage machine polishing
  • Swirl mark, scratch, and oxidation removal
  • Gloss enhancement and clarity restoration
  • Final panel wipe and inspection under lighting
  • Optional: ceramic coating add-on at completion
Multi-Stage Correction

Two or more polishing stages using a combination of cutting compound and finishing polish. Used on heavily swirled or scratched paint — delivering the most dramatic transformation possible.

Correction + Ceramic

Correct the paint first, then lock in the results with a ceramic coating. The most complete protection package — a flawless finish sealed for years.

Motorcycle Detailing

Motorcycles demand a different kind of patience. Tight spaces, intricate chrome, exposed hardware, and complex bodywork require specialized technique and tools. We bring the same obsessive standard we apply to every car to every bike — treating your motorcycle's finish with the respect it deserves. From Harley-Davidsons to sport bikes to custom builds, we have the experience to make it shine.

A proper motorcycle detail isn't just a wash and a wipe-down. Chrome and polished metal need specific compounds to remove oxidation and restore mirror clarity. Spokes, calipers, and engine fins require careful hand work. Leather seats and saddlebags need cleaning and conditioning to prevent cracking and fading. We approach every bike the same way we approach every car — methodically, with the right product for every surface. Ceramic coating is also available for motorcycle paint and chrome, dramatically cutting down on maintenance and keeping your bike looking freshly detailed all season long.

What's Included

  • Full wash and decontamination (bike-safe process)
  • Chrome and polished metal polishing
  • Engine bay and frame cleaning
  • Bodywork paint correction (if applicable)
  • Wheel and spoke detailing
  • Seat and leather surface conditioning
  • Brake dust and road grime removal
  • Optional: ceramic coating for paint & chrome
Motorcycle Detail

Complete cleaning and detailing of all surfaces — paint, chrome, leather, frame, and wheels. Perfect for show prep or bringing a neglected bike back to its best.

Motorcycle Ceramic Coating

Interior Detailing

The interior of your vehicle is where you spend the most time — and it shows more wear than any exterior surface. Our interior detailing service is a thorough, methodical process that cleans deep into every surface, eliminates embedded grime, neutralizes odors at the source, and restores the look and feel of your cabin. We treat leather, fabric, alcantara, vinyl, and all trim materials with the appropriate products and techniques — nothing generic, nothing rushed.

We start with a full vacuum of every seat, carpet panel, mat, and trunk — including the crevices most people never touch. Steam is used to sanitize hard surfaces, vents, door panels, and tight spaces without saturating materials. Leather gets properly cleaned and conditioned to prevent drying and cracking. Fabric seats and carpets are shampooed and extracted, not just wiped down. For vehicles with persistent odors — smoke, pets, mildew — we offer a targeted odor-neutralizing treatment that eliminates the source rather than covering it up. The result is a cabin that genuinely feels renewed, not just cleaned.

  • Carpet and floor mat deep shampooing & extraction
  • Dashboard, console, and door panel cleaning
  • Headliner spot cleaning
  • Interior glass cleaning (streak-free)
  • Odor elimination and deodorizer treatment
  • Plastic and rubber trim protection coat

Exterior Detailing

Your vehicle's exterior is its first impression — and it takes the full force of the elements every single day. Our exterior detail is a precision-focused service that goes far beyond a standard wash. We decontaminate, clay bar, and thoroughly clean every painted panel, piece of glass, wheel, and trim surface — leaving your vehicle with a deep, flawless finish that lasts. Whether you're prepping for a show or simply want to restore that just-delivered look, this is the service that delivers.

The process starts with a foam cannon pre-wash to loosen and lift surface contamination before we ever touch the paint, followed by a thorough hand wash and spot-free rinse. Iron fallout remover and a clay bar then pull the embedded contaminants that a wash alone can't remove — the stuff that makes paint feel rough and dull. Wheels and wheel wells get a dedicated deep clean with the appropriate brushes and chemicals. Tires are dressed, door jambs are wiped clean, and all exterior glass is cleaned and inspected. We finish with a quality inspection under professional lighting to ensure every surface looks exactly as it should.

What's Included

  • Hand wash with foam cannon and spot-free rinse
  • Iron fallout removal and clay bar decontamination
  • Wheel, tire, and wheel well deep cleaning
  • Exterior glass cleaning and water spot removal
  • Paint inspection under professional lighting
  • Trim restoration
  • Tire dressing and door jamb detailing
  • Engine bay cleaning
  • Final wipe-down and quality inspection
Exterior Detail
